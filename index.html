WHAT AM I ABOUT?

I studied pure and applied mathematics, and secondary education, before making the switch the development and engineering. It is my belief that my skills as a developer are firmly rooted in my mathematical and logical problem solving abilities. I think of problem solving as uncovering a hidden set of steps from an issue, to a solution. A developer may uncover many sets of stairs that lead to a solution, and many more that lead nowhere. Finding the "best" solution depends on the situation, and it is my job to find the most appropriate staircase for each individual circumstance.

The majority of my experience is based in Rails 5, several modern JavaScript libraries, including React, THREE.js, p5.js, and JQUERY; with substantial HTML5, CSS3, and many CSS frameworks, such as Materialize and Jekyll. Programming languages are all built on the same structures: variables, data structures, and algorithms. The only barriers involve syntax and best practices; barriers that can easily be overcome with practice, documentation, and a healthy dose of Google searches.

I have worked on projects involving social media, music, graphics, and public APIs. I am always interested in working in new fields, languages, frameworks, and methodologies!
